Our Story

A journey of compassion, courage, and connection. Discover how Spirit Animals Sanctuary came to life.

Where It All Began

Spirit Animals Sanctuary was born out of a deep love for animals and a desire to give them the life they deserve. In a quiet corner of Bucovina, Romania, what started as a dream soon grew into a haven for over 90 rescued animals—each with their own story, struggles, and spark.

Building a Sanctuary

Brick by brick, with help from volunteers and donors, the sanctuary took shape. It became a space of healing, where animals once destined for slaughter found safety, freedom, and unconditional love.

A Growing Family

Today, we’re more than a sanctuary—we’re a movement. With the support of over 11,000 people, we continue to rescue, rehabilitate, and advocate for animals across Romania and beyond. Our story is far from over—it's just getting started.
